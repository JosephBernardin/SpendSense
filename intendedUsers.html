Intended Users

Students

Students have many things to keep track of like tests, homework, commuting, and more. Let’s not make money one of them. With SpendSense, students can:

  • Track income
  • Track spending
  • Save up for their tuition

With SpendSense keeping track of their financials, students can keep their focus on their studies.

Avid Shoppers / High Spenders

Avid Shoppers and high spenders see what they want and buy what they want. Buying frequently and expensively. They can use SpendSense to:

  • Track and monitor spending habits
  • Prevent overspending

SpendSense assists avid shoppers and high spenders in their frequent spendings and reminds them that even their wallets need a break.

Average Adults

The average adults have many important expenses and things to save for. SpendSense is here to help. Adults can use SpendSense to:

  • Track income and expenses
  • Create saving goals for future investments

SpendSense helps the average adult make the overwhelming part of adulting—finances—simple.
